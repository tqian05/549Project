  • Touch & Activate

    • Touch & Activate is a novel touch sensing technology published by researchers from the University of Tsukuba in Japan for UIST’13. Their technology centers around the concept of acoustic sensing, taking advantage of the fact that solid objects have different resonant profiles when interfered with a human touching it.

    We want to build on this idea to actually define the position of the touch.

  • Touche

    Touché is a similar technology built by Disney Research. It uses the idea of reading capacitive sensing when humans touch objects.

    Touché uses Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing, which differs from our product and has a higher cost
